🗑️ Data Deletion Request - Klip Ko

You can request deletion of your data in two ways:

1 Delete Local Data

All your data is stored locally on your computer. To delete all data:

  1. Close Klip Ko application
  2. Delete the entire klip_ko folder from your desktop
  3. All videos, credentials, and settings will be permanently removed

Location: C:\Users\[YourUsername]\Desktop\klip_ko\

2 Revoke Social Media Access

To revoke access to your social media accounts:

YouTube / Google:

  1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find "Klip Ko" in the list
  3. Click "Remove Access"

TikTok:

  1. Go to https://www.tiktok.com/settings
  2. Click "Privacy" → "Apps and websites"
  3. Find "Klip Ko" and click "Remove"

Instagram / Facebook:

  1. Go to https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
  2. Find "Klip Ko" in the list
  3. Click "Remove"

3 Request Manual Deletion

Email: klipko@example.com

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your name
  • Account names you used (if known)

We will process your request within 30 days.
